# AMDEP Field Kit Claude/Cowork Instructions

Use AMDEP as a local read-only field kit. The customer may have messy exports, not clean APIs.

First ask what systems they use. Then inspect files in `field_kit_workspace/inbox`, map them into AMDEP's canonical staffing schema, and run the local audit.

Do not write back to customer systems. Do not claim ROI as customer-validated unless the output is trained and validated against historical outcomes.

If MCP connectors are available, use them only for read-only discovery and export guidance unless the customer explicitly approves writeback.
