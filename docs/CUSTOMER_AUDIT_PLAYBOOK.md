# Customer Audit Playbook

## Sales Wedge

Send us your last 30 days of assignments, personnel home ZIPs, jobsite addresses, skills, certifications, and supervisor assignments. AmDep returns a waste map, dispatch delta, burden-risk table, and savings estimate.

## Who To Start With

Priority targets:

1. roofing and restoration operators
2. storm response contractors
3. utility vegetation and restoration contractors
4. telecom/fiber construction subs
5. industrial services branches
6. energy services teams
7. commercial HVAC as a later adjacency

## Required Data

Minimum:

- personnel roster
- home ZIPs or coordinates
- active and historical jobsites
- assignment history for 30 days
- skills
- certifications
- supervisor assignments
- crew assignments
- vehicles and equipment

Better:

- overtime history
- late arrivals
- callbacks
- rework
- inspection outcomes
- field notes
- photo timestamps
- weather
- delay reasons
- turnover outcomes

## Diagnostic Deliverables

- dispatch replay of current assignments
- optimized counterfactual schedule
- calibration gym result
- recommended optimizer weights
- deployment map
- route burn hours/day
- top avoidable bad deployments
- closer qualified alternatives
- supervisor overload list
- certification bottleneck list
- personnel burden table
- job staffing status
- robotics readiness candidates
- conservative/base/aggressive ROI estimate

## Pricing

- $10k-$25k diagnostic for smaller contractor
- $25k-$75k diagnostic for mid-market operator
- $75k-$150k implementation
- $3k-$20k/month analytics retainer
- enterprise pricing for multi-region operators

## Audit Call Script

We are not asking you to replace your dispatch system.

Give us the last 30 days of where people actually went, who they report to, what they are qualified to do, and where the jobs were. We will show where travel, certification bottlenecks, supervisor spread, and crew fragmentation cost money.

If the map does not find real waste, you should not buy the larger product.
